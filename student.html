Idea Ownership & Legal Protection

Copyright Act, 1957 – India

According to the Copyright Act, 1957, all original intellectual creations including literary works, software, artwork, and other forms of content are legally protected in India. This act ensures these are secured by law and cannot be copied.

By submitting their projects and innovative ideas on StartupCollab, users should share creatives as confidential and remain the intellectual property of the respective creators.
