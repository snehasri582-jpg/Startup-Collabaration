HealthTech Platform

🏥 HealthTech 📞 Telemedicine 💻 SaaS

HealthTech Platform is a modern telemedicine solution that connects doctors and patients through instant digital consultations and continuous health monitoring.

The platform enables patients to consult certified medical professionals remotely, reducing wait times and improving access to healthcare services.

Doctors can manage appointments, access patient history, and provide real-time medical advice using secure communication channels.

Integrated health monitoring tools allow patients to track vitals and share data seamlessly with healthcare providers.

Our mission is to make healthcare accessible, efficient, and technology-driven.

🔗 Series: Seed

Core Features

📞 Instant Consultations

Patients can connect with doctors instantly via video, voice, or chat consultations from anywhere.

📊 Health Monitoring

Track vital signs and health data continuously, enabling proactive care and early diagnosis.
